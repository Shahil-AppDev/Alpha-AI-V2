- **Risk**: Illegal mobile device monitoring
- **Legal Status**: Violates telecommunications privacy laws

**Audio/Video Surveillance**
- MicrophoneSpy, WebcamSpy, AudioRecorder, VoiceSpy
- **Risk**: Invasion of privacy, illegal recording
- **Legal Status**: Illegal without consent in most jurisdictions
